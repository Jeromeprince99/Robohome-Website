How We Do

Core Intelligence

Robohome's core Intelligence lets the robots to learn common abilities such as robust 3D perception, physical affordances of objects, few-shot learning and real-time motion planning. This allows them to adapt to new tasks just like people do — by breaking down complex tasks into simple steps and applying general skills to complete them.

Flawless Integration

We’re building more robust software. If one component lags, the whole system fails. This prevents malfunctioning of the robot. Our robust software integrates all components flawlessly and gives seamless operations between all components.
